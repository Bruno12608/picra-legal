# Picra — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: [29 June 2026]**

This Privacy Policy explains how **Picra** ("Picra", "we", "us") handles information when you use the
Picra iOS app. By using the app you agree to this policy.

Contact: **[brunoschuermans@icloud.com]**

## Information we process

- **Photos you choose to enhance.** When you tap Enhance, the selected photo is sent securely to our
  processing service and to our AI providers to generate the enhanced result. Photos are used **only**
  to produce your edited image and to provide the service. We do not use your photos for advertising
  and we do not sell them.
- **Photos you save locally.** Your created images are stored **on your device** (in the app's
  library) and, if you choose, saved to your Photos. We do not upload your library.
- **A username.** A display name is generated on your device (e.g. "@picra_1234") and can be changed
  by you. It is stored on your device.
- **Usage data.** We keep a local count of how many enhancements you've made to enforce plan limits.
- **Purchase information.** Subscriptions are processed by **Apple**; we receive only the subscription
  status, never your payment details.
- **Diagnostics.** The app may log non-personal technical events (e.g. "enhancement succeeded/failed")
  to help us fix bugs.

We do **not** require an account, and we do not collect your name, email, contacts, or location.

## AI processing and third parties

To enhance your photo, the image is sent to these processors:

- **OpenAI** — analyzes the photo to decide the enhancement. See openai.com privacy policy.
- **Replicate** (and the AI models it hosts, e.g. ByteDance Seedream) — performs the image edit. See
  replicate.com privacy policy.
- Requests are routed through our **proxy hosted on Cloudflare**, which forwards them and does not
  retain your images.

These providers process your photo to return a result. Their handling and retention of data are
governed by their own privacy policies. We instruct providers to process data only to deliver the
service. **Do not upload photos you do not have the right to process.**

## Data retention

- Enhanced images live **on your device** until you delete them.
- Photos sent for processing are transient — used to generate your result and not stored by us. Our
  AI providers may retain inputs/outputs temporarily per their policies.

## Your choices and rights

- **Delete your data:** delete individual creations in the Gallery, or delete the app to remove all
  locally stored data (images, username, usage counts).
- **Manage your subscription:** in iOS Settings → your name → Subscriptions.
- Depending on your location (e.g. EEA/UK/California) you may have rights to access or delete personal
  data. Contact us at the email above.

## Children

Picra is not directed to children under [13 / 16]. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy; we'll change the "Last updated" date above and, for significant changes,
notify you in the app.
